Who We Are

Beijing Jiyou Decoration Engineering

Beijing Jiyou Decoration Engineering Co., Ltd was established in 2010 with a vision to provide exceptional interior decoration and design services to clients worldwide. Headquartered in Beijing, China, we have grown from a small local company to an internationally recognized provider of comprehensive decoration and construction solutions.

Our registered address at Room 407-4245, Shilibao Town Government Office Building, No. 67 Xidaqiao Road, Miyun District, Beijing, serves as our operational headquarters, from where we manage projects across the globe.

In recent years, we have expanded our services to include innovative mobile applications available on both Google Play and the Apple App Store, enabling our clients to manage their projects more efficiently and stay connected with our team at all times.
