Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

Beijing Jiyou Decoration Engineering Co., Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://bjjiyou.com, use our mobile applications available on Google Play and the Apple App Store, or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and postal address
  • Payment information and billing details
  • Project specifications and preferences
  • Communication history and feedback
  • Account credentials and profile information

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website or use our applications, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device type, operating system, and browser information
  • IP address and geographic location
  • Pages visited and time spent on our services
  • Referring website or application
  • Usage patterns and preferences

1.3 Mobile Application Data

Our mobile applications may collect additional information to enhance your experience:

  • Device identifiers and advertising identifiers
  • App usage data and feature interactions
  • Project-related data and uploaded content
  • Camera and photo access (for design visualization features)
  • Push notification preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and customer service requests
  • Communicate with you about products, services, and events
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activities
  • Personalize and improve your experience

3.2 Types of Advertisements We Display

Our applications may display the following types of advertisements:

  • Splash Ads: Full-screen advertisements displayed when the application launches
  • Rewarded Video Ads: Video advertisements that users can watch to receive in-app rewards or benefits
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen advertisements displayed between content or at natural transition points
  • Banner Ads: Rectangular advertisements displayed at the top or bottom of the screen
  • Native Ads: Advertisements designed to match the visual style of the application content

3.3 Managing Ad Preferences

You can manage your advertising preferences in the following ways:

  • Device Settings: Use the "Opt Out of Ads Personalization" feature in your device settings
  • Google Play Services: On Android devices, you can reset your advertising identifier and opt out of personalized ads through Google Settings
  • Apple Settings: On iOS devices, enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in Settings > Privacy > Advertising
  • In-App Settings: Some of our applications offer in-app settings to manage ad preferences

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating our business
  • Advertising partners for ad delivery and optimization
  • Analytics providers to understand service usage
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business partners with your consent

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. We will delete or anonymize your data when it is no longer needed.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including Standard Contractual Clauses or compliance with applicable data protection laws.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@bjjiyou.com.

10. Regional Privacy Laws Compliance

10.1 GDPR (European Union)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, we process your data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation. You have additional rights including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

10.2 CCPA (California)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information.

10.3 COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection)

We comply with COPPA requirements. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent. Our applications are not designed to attract children.

10.4 GDPR-K (South Korea)

For users in South Korea, we comply with the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) and the GDPR-K requirements.

10.5 PIPEDA (Canada)

We comply with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) for users in Canada.

10.6 LGPD (Brazil)

For users in Brazil, we process personal data in accordance with Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD).

10.7 PDPA (Thailand)

We comply with Thailand's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) for users in Thailand.

10.8 Privacy Act (Australia)

For Australian users, we comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles.

10.9 POPIA (South Africa)

We comply with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

10.10 PDPA (Singapore)

We comply with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

11. Third-Party Links

Our services may contain links to third-party websites or applications. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
