FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical decoration project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A standard residential project typically takes 4-12 weeks, while larger commercial projects may take several months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer complimentary initial consultations to discuss your project requirements, preferences, and budget. Contact us to schedule your free consultation today.

What areas do you serve?

While based in Beijing, we serve clients globally. Our mobile apps allow us to effectively manage projects and communicate with clients regardless of location.

Are your services available for international clients?

Absolutely! We have extensive experience working with international clients and can accommodate different time zones and communication preferences. Our mobile applications make remote collaboration seamless.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfer, credit cards, and online payment platforms. Payment terms are discussed during the project planning phase.
